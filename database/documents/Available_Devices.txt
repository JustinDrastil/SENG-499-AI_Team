The following is a list of all of the devices in Cambridge Bay that have data.

The **deviceCode** values can be used as a filter on the [locations](/spaces/O2A/pages/49447546/locations+Discovery+Service), [properties](/spaces/O2A/pages/48696426/properties+Discovery+Service), [dataProducts](/spaces/O2A/pages/48696428/dataProducts+Discovery+Service) and [dataProductDelivery](/spaces/O2A/pages/48696520/dataProductDelivery+Service) services. Filters on the **deviceCode** and **deviceName** columns can be used to reduce the results returned from the [devices](https://wiki.oceannetworks.ca/display/help/devices+Discovery+Service) service. The **deviceLink** column provides a link to the Device Listing page for the specific device.
